The Odin Project: Recipes Project

Summer is here! You know what that means.. SMOOTHIES! Now what is Summer without them? Sure, we have our lovely lemonades and our other favorite beverages, but nothing beats a refreshing smoothie by the pool, campfire, beach, barbecue grill, Grandma's house, or even after a great workout session. You get the idea.

What I specifically love about these recipes is that they are for those who are lactose intolerant! Okay, I know what you're thinking, but hear me out. As I've gotten older, I ended up becoming a member of the lactose team. Having to adjust my dairy intake and make crucial changes to my diet, has become quite challenging for me. In the beginning of something new, it took me awhile to accept the fact that I have to change my ways because everything that I liked always caused an upset stomach, and being stubborn I had to suffer the consequences afterwards. Never would I wish this on anyone!

Growing up in a big family, me being number 7 out of 18, it was rare to be lactose. As for me being lucky number 7, I out of everyone in this family became a member of the lactose intolerant team. Until, one of my older sisters gave birth to her eldest child, however she became lactose at a much earlier age. (That's another story, but I don't want to lose track of why I'm creating this! Please stick with me!) Anyways, as I was looking for ways to enhance my taste for what I grew up loving, it took me a while to get used to this change. What assured, or rather "awaken" my senses to accept that I can't have what I love, is the consequences that I've unfortunately endured after devouring a delicious homemade meal. Again I would never wish this on anyone! With my stubbornness to add to the journey of being lactose intolerant, I finally gave in and started doing my research!

I know there are a lot of different recipes that I could've chosen that were vegan, or lactose-free, however I've decided to put together my top 3 favorite smoothie recipes and yes, one including our furry friends too! (A favorite that our family dog actually enjoyed the most - hence being a picky eater!) Thinking at the exact time that I'm creating this, I thought it would be only fit to create a webpage for what's fitting for the time of year, SUMMER!

Note: All recipes were inspired from helpful friends. If you have enjoyed these recipes, like I have, I have included a direct link to each smoothie recipe. If you're on the same boat as myself, or currently entering the lactose team - Welcome! Or, if you're just looking to change your diet, or just finding better recipes to enhance your liking for food or beverages, each direct link has their own lists of recommendations, homemade meals and beverages!(Highly Encouraged!)

I hope you enjoyed a little bit of content about myself, and I thank you for stopping by to read! Each recipe has confirmed to be my personal top 3, and each recipe is quick, simple, and definitely delicious.

Enjoy, stay cool, and take care of yourselves!
